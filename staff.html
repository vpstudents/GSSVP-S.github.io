Governance

GSS Council

The GSS Council is the primary governing body of the Society, made up of elected departmental graduate student representatives, the executive team, and graduate student senators.

Monthly Council Meetings

Council meets on the third Thursday of every month in the Michael Kingsmill Forum (Room 4301) in the AMS Student Nest, from 5:30 – 7:30 pm. All meetings are open to graduate students and honorary members to attend. Contact the GSS President to request an invitation if you are not a graduate student.
