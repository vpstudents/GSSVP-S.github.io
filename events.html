AO Events

Hosting Events as an AO

Each Affiliate Organization receives up to $1,000 per academic year for social, cultural, academic, and professional events. Here's how the reimbursement process works.

Event Funding Steps

1

Download forms — Get the Event Fund Application, Budget Spreadsheet, and (if needed) Waiver Form from the GSS website.

2

Submit to VP Students — Email completed documents to vpstudents@gss.ubc.ca before your event.

3

Get approval — The VP Students will confirm the approved funding amount.

4

Hold your event — Collect attendance sheets, receipts, and photos/videos for social media. Note: AO funds cannot cover alcohol.

5

Submit post-event report — Complete the Post-Event Report, compile receipts into a single PDF (under 10MB), and email to vpstudents@gss.ubc.ca within two weeks to receive reimbursement.
