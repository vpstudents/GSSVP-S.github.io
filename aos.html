Affiliated Organizations

Over 50 graduate organizations partnered with the GSS, gaining access to funding, event promotion, and campus-wide collaboration.

What AOs get

Benefits of Affiliation

As a GSS Affiliate Organization, your group gains access to resources and support that help you engage the broader graduate community at UBC.

📢 Newsletter & social promotion 💰 Up to $1,000 event funding/year 🤝 Collaborative event opportunities 🎓 Leadership workshops 📡 AO Discord community

How to Become an AO

1

Complete the Application Form — Find the AO application form on gss.ubc.ca and email it to both vpstudents@gss.ubc.ca and aa@gss.ubc.ca.

2

Gather Supporting Signatures — Include four supporting signatures plus the primary contact (use an official email, not personal).

3

Confirmation Email — Expect a reply within a week. If not, follow up — emails can occasionally be missed.

4

Attend Council Meeting — Attend the next Council meeting to address any questions. If no objections, your affiliation is approved!
